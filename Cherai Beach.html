Cherai Beach

Around two dozen kilometres from the industrial district of Ernakulam and to the side of the Vypeen Island lies every swimmer’s paradise, Cherai Beach. It provides a wonderful view of the famous Chinese Fishing Nets or Cheena Vala well. The nearby shacks provide you with fresh cuisine that fills you up perfectly after a delightful swim. The area itself has seen many new resorts and hotels crop up, which have facilitated the increase in tourist activity. Cherai Beach has a 10 km long coastline that is clean and sandy, perfect for sunbathing, swimming and surfing. The best time to visit cherai beach is during the evenings, to watch a gorgeous sunset.
