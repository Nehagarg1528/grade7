Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary

Experience nature in its full swing at Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary,Idukki. A place where you get to see an abundance of flora and fauna along with mind-blowing peaks, the Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary offers a laid-back atmosphere to tourists of all ages and presents a perfect weekend getaway to explore the popular wildlife habitat of India. The wildlife along with the deciduous trees makes it an ideal spot for trekking.Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary is also home to the Great Grizzled Squirrel of India. Numbering less than 200, they are among the most endangered species on the planet.
