Kovalam Beach

Kovalam beach is actually a long stretch of white sandy beach running alongside the Malabar Coast. The three separated parts of the beach are Hawah beach, Samudra beach and the Light House beach. The largest among these three beaches is the Light House beach which is a must visit beach of Kovalam. A massive rocky promontory on the Kovalam beach has created a beautiful bay of calm waters ideal for sea bathing.The leisure options at this beach are plenty and diverse.Sunbathing, swimming, herbal body toning massages, special cultural programmes and catamaran cruising are some of them.
