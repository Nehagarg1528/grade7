Museum Of Kerala History

Museum Of Kerala History will take you on mystical ride through Kerala’s rich past,present and even a glimpse of the future. You will experience the stories of people and events that changed the fortunes of this lush land.In order to learn more about Kerala’s significant contribution to the history of the region and the world,be sure to take a Museum Tour.
